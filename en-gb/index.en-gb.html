About me

I obtained a MSc in Computer Science from Cergy-Pontoise University, France, and a MSc in Electronics from the French Engineering School ENSEA both in 2016. I did my last-year internship at Vilmorin-Mikado (Limagrain Group) on computer vision and machine learning applied to maize seed varieties classification and disease detection. I got a Ph.D. in Computer Science (Computer Vision and Deep Learning) at CY Paris University under the supervision of Aymeric Histace (ETIS), David Picard (Ecole des Ponts ParisTech) and Edouard Klein (French National Police Forces).
